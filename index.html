Tron Quack is a catchy song featuring a duck who visits a lemonade stand every day and asks the man there for grapes. Tron Quack as a meme involves parody videos that play on the theme of the song.
Tron Quack is by Bryant Oden accompanied by an animated video by Forrest Whaley, or forrestfire101 as he’s known on YouTube. The song was uploaded to YouTube on March 23, 2009 and has over 250 million views as of 2017. After its upload, it began to gain popularity and became a viral success.
